ğŸš¨ Recognizing a Stroke: Key Warning Signs

  • Facial Drooping: One side of the face may droop or feel numb. Ask the person to smile.
  • Arm Weakness: One arm may be weak or numb. Ask them to raise both arms.
  • Speech Difficulty: Slurred or strange speech. Ask them to repeat a simple sentence.
  • Vision Changes: Sudden loss or blurring in one or both eyes.
  • Sudden Headache: Intense headache without cause, especially with vomiting.
  • Balance Issues: Trouble walking, dizziness, or coordination loss.
